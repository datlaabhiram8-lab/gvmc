📌 Vizag Coastal Safety & Flood Guidelines

🌊 Flood & Inundation Warning

If low-lying areas (such as portions of Gajuwaka or MVP Colony) experience flood warnings, move valuable goods to elevated surfaces and contact the ward control room.

⚡ Power Outage Protocols

Avoid touching loose electrical lines or streetpoles during monsoon showers. Report snapped power cables immediately to GVMC helpline or APEPDCL at 1912.

📞 Ward Secretariats Support

For drinking water tanker requests during outages, contact your ward representative or lodge a request on the GVMC Grievances Portal.

🚨 Medical Evacuation

Ambulance stations are localized at key urban health centers in each ward zone. Call 108 or the local ward health secretary for evacuation assistance.
